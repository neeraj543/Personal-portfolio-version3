Internship Observation

Diamond Administration India Pvt Ltd is a subsidiary of XSS BVBA, a Belgian software company that has been serving the diamond industry for over 25 years. The company has clients in Belgium, Hong Kong, Dubai, and Thailand.

The team in India consists of 11 employees, while the Belgium office has 5. During my observation, I learned about their Bidding Project — a website for estimating and bidding on polished stones in a tender.
